I want to improve my mapping skills in R. The Australian Bureou of Statistics (ABS) are now preparing spatial data in an open format called GeoPackage, so I thought I would write a post on how I used an ABS dataset to prepare a map. Just a quick word of warning: I plodded through this, so I will try and point out some of the traps I had to work through, but this is not a master class and my terminology is not going to necessarily align with people that know what they are talking about.

In this post I will cover off on:

  • where I sourced the data;
  • which R packages I used;
  • how I worked in my own data;
  • things I learned along the way;
  • problems that I needed to overcome.
